Milestones & Breakthroughs

Our journey has been marked by strategic vision, adaptability to economic shifts, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Here are some of our key achievements that have defined our path.

STRATEGIC DEVELOPMENT

Business Optimization

Through continuous study of market trends, we are always developing new business varieties and optimizing our operational structure. This proactive approach clarifies our company's direction and goals, ensuring we stay ahead in a dynamic market.

SCOPE EXPANSION

First Major Trade

In a major breakthrough for our business scope, we initiated the export of asphalt mixtures and fuel oil. This culminated in the successful completion of our first major trade, delivering 500,000 barrels.

PARTNERSHIPS

Market Development

We successfully developed markets for fuel oil and bitumen mixture in key regions like Shandong and Northeast China. This expansion was solidified by establishing deals with major partners such as PanjinYijiu Petrochemical and Baolai Bioenergy.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Export Achievement

A significant operational milestone was achieved with our infrastructure supporting a monthly export volume of approximately 600,000 tons. This accomplishment showcases our capacity for large-scale, reliable delivery.

Our approach to sustainability

At Falco Corporation, we embrace innovation, sustainability, and excellence in every aspect of our business.

A sustainable approach is essential to how we operate. As we navigate the global energy transition, we recognize the scale and urgency of the climate challenge and are committed to playing our part in the shift towards a lower-carbon economy.

It allows the Company to consider how societal and environmental issues affect our day-to-day business, and is a key driver of our strategy as we innovate for the long-term health of our company and the planet.

We are leveraging our operational strengths and business portfolio to play our part in the energy transition, underpinned by our ambition to support global climate goals and develop lower-carbon energy solutions.

Energy Products

We produce high-quality crude oil, fuel oil, LPG, diesel, and refined hydrocarbon products.

We continually seek new opportunities to expand and diversify our energy products portfolio, with the intent of creating additional value and offering innovative solutions to our customers around the world.

We deliver high-quality products via our dedicated system, to our growing global customer base. Our activities consist primarily of refining and manufacturing, providing high-value products domestically and internationally.

From advanced transportation fuels to industrial feedstocks and lubricants, our energy and chemicals range aims to create value not only for our company, but also for our partners and shareholders.
